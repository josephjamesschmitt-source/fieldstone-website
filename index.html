About Us

We Partner with Business Owners to Steward What They've Built

Fieldstone Group is supported by experienced advisors and a network of investors who share our long-term approach. We are looking to acquire a business, run it ourselves, and hold it for the long term, keeping the brand, the team, and the culture intact.

We grew up in the communities we're targeting. Our families still live there. That's not a talking point; it's why we're drawn to businesses in these markets and why we take seriously the responsibility of stewarding something an owner has spent years building.

We are not financial engineers or absentee investors. We're looking for one owner who is ready to transition and wants confidence that their business will be in good hands long after they've stepped away.

Our Commitment

For many owners, the decision to sell is one of the hardest they'll make. We respect that. Our goal is to make the process as straightforward as possible and to be the kind of buyers an owner feels good about handing the keys to.

Our Philosophy

Our Approach

We take a different approach to acquisition — one rooted in respect for the businesses and people we work with.

Legacy Preservation

Your business identity, culture, and team matter to us. We don't rebrand, relocate, or restructure for the sake of it.

Long-Term Stewardship

We acquire to operate, not to flip. We're building for the long term, not a three-year exit.

Hands-On Operators

We work in the business, not just on it, building direct relationships with the people running your company, every day.

Flexible Partnership

Every transition is different. We work with sellers on terms that respect their timeline and goals.

What We Look For

Investment Criteria

We are seeking established, profitable businesses with strong fundamentals and a track record of consistent performance.

EBITDA $750K – $1.25M
Revenue $5M – $20M
Geography Midwest & Mid-Atlantic
Operating History 10+ Years Preferred

Target Industries

Home Services

HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, and other essential residential and commercial service businesses with recurring revenue and strong local reputations.

Value-Added Distribution

Specialty distributors that go beyond logistics, providing technical expertise, custom solutions, or value-added services to their customers.

B2B Services

Professional and commercial services businesses with sticky customer relationships, consistent demand, and opportunities for operational improvement.

We are open to other industries with similar characteristics and are happy to have a conversation regardless of sector.

Meet the Team

Joe Schmitt

Co-Founder

A Wisconsin native and graduate of Northeastern University, he began his career at PwC advising on M&A transactions across healthcare and pharmaceuticals before joining Kohler Company to build an internal venture targeting emerging markets, driving market entry across nine countries. He co-founded Pitch-Partners, a marketing services firm supporting real estate transactions. He is focused on acquiring and operating a business where he can apply his experience, grow his family, and put down roots in a community he cares about.
